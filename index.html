What I do

UX/UI design

I create digital experiences that are both aesthetically pleasing and intuitive to use. I conduct user research to gain insight into the needs and goals of the end-users, and then use that information to create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups. My ultimate goal is to design products that are not only functional, but also delightful to use.

Front-end

I bring designs to life through the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I strive to create responsive and accessible web pages that meet the needs of both users and the design team. I am experienced in a variety of frontend frameworks, including React, Angular, and Vue.js, and I am constantly seeking to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest web technologies.

Back-end

I build the server-side of applications. I create REST APIs and work with databases to store and retrieve data. I have experience with various backend languages and frameworks, including Node.js and Python. My goal is to create scalable and efficient systems that support the needs of the frontend and provide a seamless user experience.
