Our Way - Mobile solutions

5th Semester Project

OurWay has been designed with senior inhabitants of a little village. By connecting people to neighbours who are going the same way, anyone can carpool with a trusted driver at any time. It improves social cohesion for all age groups and prevents mobility issues and loneliness

At Windesheim University, OurWay can beused to connect students and/or employees to travel together. It is a sustainable solution to the current parking issues at the campus. www.ourway-app.nl.
