KapasaVisualization of
welfare study data

Internship

Students' welfare plays a big role in how much they get out of their education. Monitoring students' welfare will help in early detection of mental illnesses, inadequate learning environments or domestic problems. Other previous research has focused on jobs, their workers and their welfare and tried to create an individually personalized feedback application . This showed some 1 success in improving workers welfare. However, it was time consuming and required willingness and honesty. As data for visualization this project uses hypothetical test data randomly generated in order to simulate all possible welfare situations. The focus is not on what the data is but how it is represented and interpreted. The data is shown in simple intuitive graphs in order to make it easier for people without a mathematical background to understand it. Visualizing welfare data was found to be very difficult. The wish of representing accurate detailed data without making it too complex for the user to interpret what the data’s meaning.
